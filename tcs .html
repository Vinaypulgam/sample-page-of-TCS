Tata Consultancy Services Limited (TCS) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Mumbai.[7][8] It is a part of the Tata Group and operates in 150 locations across 46 countries.[9] In March 2024, it was reported that TCS had more than 601,546 employees worldwide.[10][11] TCS is the second-largest Indian company by market capitalization, the most valuable IT service brands worldwide, and the top Big Tech (India) company.[12][13][14] In 2012, it was the world's second-largest user of U.S. H-1B visas.[15] As of 2021, it was ranked seventh on the Fortune India 500 list.[16] In September 2021, TCS recorded a market capitalization of US$200 billion, making it the first Indian IT tech company to do so.[17][18][19][20][21] In 2016â€“2017, parent company Tata Sons owned 72.05% of TCS[22] and more than 70% of Tata Sons' dividends were generated by TCS.
